🧮 Aptitude

Aptitude refers to a natural ability to learn, solve problems, and apply logic. In academics and career preparation, aptitude skills include **quantitative reasoning, logical reasoning, verbal ability, and data interpretation**. Practicing aptitude helps in improving problem-solving speed, analytical skills, and decision-making abilities.
